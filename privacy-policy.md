# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: July 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how ThePoint QB Connector, operated by 3-102-948878 SRL under the trade name Habitat Royal, accesses, processes, and protects financial and accounting data.

## 1. Purpose

ThePoint QB Connector is an internal financial data integration and reporting tool designed to retrieve information from QuickBooks Online for financial analysis, reporting, cost monitoring, and business intelligence dashboards.

## 2. Information Accessed

The application may access financial and accounting information available through an authorized QuickBooks Online company, including:

- Chart of accounts
- Financial reports
- Accounting transactions
- Invoices and payments
- Bills and expenses
- Vendors and customers
- Account balances and financial metrics

The application only accesses information necessary for financial reporting and analysis.

## 3. Read-Only Data Use

ThePoint QB Connector is designed for read-only financial data processing.

The application does not intentionally create, modify, delete, or alter accounting records or transactions within QuickBooks Online.

Data retrieved from QuickBooks Online is used to prepare internal reports, financial statements, analytical models, cost metrics, and business intelligence dashboards.

## 4. Data Sharing and Sale

3-102-948878 SRL does not sell financial or accounting data accessed through ThePoint QB Connector.

Financial data is not shared with unauthorized third parties.

Information may only be accessed by authorized personnel or service providers when necessary for legitimate financial, administrative, or technical purposes.

## 5. Data Security

Reasonable administrative and technical measures are used to protect financial information and authentication credentials.

Access to QuickBooks Online data is managed through authorized authentication mechanisms provided by Intuit.

Authentication credentials, access tokens, and other sensitive information are not intended to be publicly disclosed.

## 6. Data Retention

Financial data may be temporarily or permanently stored when necessary for internal financial reporting, historical analysis, and business intelligence purposes.

Access to stored information is restricted to authorized users.

## 7. Third-Party Services

ThePoint QB Connector integrates with QuickBooks Online, a service provided by Intuit Inc.

The use and processing of information by QuickBooks Online are subject to Intuit's applicable privacy policies and terms.

The application may also use business intelligence and data processing tools for internal financial analysis and reporting.

## 8. User Authorization

Access to QuickBooks Online data occurs only after authorization by an authorized user of the applicable QuickBooks Online company.

Authorization may be revoked through the applicable QuickBooks Online or Intuit account settings.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when necessary to reflect changes in the application, data processing practices, or applicable requirements.
